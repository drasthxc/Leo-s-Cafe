About us



The story we're about to tell takes place in 2018, on the streets of Asuncion in Tondo, where our store is situated. We offer freshly ground coffee beans, tea, dishes, and desserts to our customers. What began as a dream of a corporate employee has now become a reality for us. We strive to maintain the highest standards of quality and service, blending tradition and innovation to offer unique experiences to our patrons. Each cup of coffee we serve has a story behind it.


Leo’s Café, a coffee shop located in 1198 Asuncion St. Tondo, Manila, Manila. The café was established on August 9, 2018, and offers a variety of hot and cold beverages, including coffee and smoothies, as well as some food items such as burgers, carbonara, and desserts. The owner of the café is Sir Leo Richmond Arguelles. A product that sir Leo recommends is the salted caramel latte, which is known for its unique sweet and salty taste.
